MISSION OVERVIEW

The Bryan, Texas Aerospace Technology Center is an independent research and development organization established in 2026.

We focus on the advancement of small-scale rocketry and aerospace software systems. Our facility operates with a primary directive of zero environmental degradation, ensuring that the next generation of space technology is built sustainably.

Operational Focus:

  • Small-scale propulsion testing
  • High-altitude recovery systems
  • Sustainable aerospace materials
